A **free, browser-based teleprompter** for video calls, live streams, and recordings.
No installation, no sign-up, no server — just open it in your browser and start speaking.

**Live demo:** https://ozgurkurul.github.io/prompter/

---

## What is this?

Prompter is a lightweight teleprompter that runs entirely inside your browser.
Paste your script, hit play, and the text scrolls smoothly — just like a professional teleprompter.

It's designed to sit **on top of your Zoom / Google Meet / Microsoft Teams window** using
Picture-in-Picture mode, so you can read your notes naturally while looking at the camera.

---

## What does it do?

- ▶️ Smooth, adjustable auto-scrolling text with live speed & font controls.
- 🎨 Inline formatting: make words **bold** or colored right inside your script.
- 🪟 **Picture-in-Picture** window that stays on top of any meeting or stream.
- 💾 **Auto-save** — everything you type is saved instantly to your browser.
- 📁 **Profiles & Contents** — organize scripts for different events, talks or clients.
- 📤 **Export / Import** — back up or move all your scripts as a single JSON file.
- ⌨️ Full keyboard control (play, speed, font, step, profiles, fullscreen…).
- 🖥️ Works fully offline once the page is loaded. No data ever leaves your device.

---

## Who is it for?

- Speakers giving online presentations or webinars
- Content creators recording videos or podcasts
- Streamers who need cue cards during a live show
- Anyone on a video call who wants a clean, readable script always visible
