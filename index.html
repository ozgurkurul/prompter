❔ How to use Prompter

Basics

  • Paste your text into the input box and press Space (or click Start) to begin scrolling.
  • Use the Speed and Font sliders to adjust playback. They are remembered across sessions.
  • Click Save & Load to store the current text into the active content and push it to the prompter.
  • Click ⧉ Undock to open the prompter in a separate Picture-in-Picture window.

Text formatting

You can mark up parts of your text directly inside the input:

  • **bold text** → bold text
  • {red:important} → important
  • {yellow:warning} → warning
  • {#4ec9b0:accent} → accent (any hex color works)

Example:
Today we'll focus on **AI safety** and {#ffcc00:alignment}.

Profiles & Contents

  • Open the side panel with C or P, or click the ☰ Profiles & Contents button.
  • Profiles group related contents (e.g. different talks, meetings). Create/rename/delete them from the dropdown bar.
  • Contents are individual scripts inside the active profile. Drag to reorder, click to load.
  • Ctrl+← / Ctrl+→ cycle through contents within the active profile.
  • Use ⬇ Export / ⬆ Import to back up or transfer all contents as JSON.
